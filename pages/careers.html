Career Opportunities

Join the Little Burma Family

At Little Burma Café, our team is the heart of everything we do. We’re always looking for friendly, motivated people who love great food, warm hospitality, and a buzzing café atmosphere.

If you enjoy working with people and want to grow your skills in a supportive environment, we’d love to hear from you.

Current Openings

Line Cook

Dublin City Centre Full-time €15–€18/hr

We’re looking for an experienced Line Cook to help prep, cook, and plate our Burmese-inspired dishes to a high standard during busy service.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and cook menu items to recipe and presentation standards
  • Keep your station clean, organised, and safe at all times
  • Work closely with the Head Chef and kitchen team
  • Follow food safety and hygiene procedures

Requirements:

  • 1–2 years experience in a professional kitchen
  • Ability to work under pressure during busy shifts
  • Good communication and teamwork skills

Barista

Dublin City Centre Part-time / Full-time €13–€16/hr + tips

We’re looking for a friendly Barista with a love of coffee and people. You’ll be the face of the café, serving drinks and welcoming customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare hot and cold coffee drinks to a high standard
  • Take customer orders and handle payments
  • Keep the bar area clean and well stocked
  • Deliver warm, welcoming customer service

Requirements:

  • Previous barista or customer service experience (preferred)
  • Strong communication and people skills
  • Positive attitude and a willingness to learn

Front of House Server

Dublin City Centre Part-time €12–€14/hr + tips

We’re looking for energetic Servers to look after guests, take orders, and help create a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere in the café.

Responsibilities:

  • Welcome guests and show them to their tables
  • Take food and drink orders accurately
  • Serve dishes and check on guests throughout their visit
  • Work closely with the kitchen and bar teams

Requirements:

  • Strong communication and customer service skills
  • Ability to stay calm during busy periods
  • Attention to detail and a friendly attitude
