Career Opportunities

Join the Little Burma Family

At Little Burma Café, our team is the heart of everything we do. We're always looking for friendly, motivated people who love great food, warm hospitality, and a buzzing café atmosphere.

If you enjoy working with people and want to grow your skills in a supportive environment, we'd love to hear from you.

Current Openings

Line Cook

Dublin City Centre Part-time / Full-time €15–€18/hr + bonus

We're looking for motivated Line Cooks who enjoy working in a fast-paced kitchen and take pride in preparing high-quality dishes.. Responsibilities include food prep, cooking, plating, and helping keep the kitchen running smoothly during busy service hours.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare, cook, and plate menu items according to restaurant standards
  • Support kitchen operations by communicating and coordinating with the team
  • Follow all food safety and hygiene procedures
  • Maintain a clean, organized, and sanitary workstation

Requirements:

  • 1–2 years of kitchen or line cook experience preferred
  • Ability to work efficiently under pressure in a fast-paced environment
  • Knowledge of basic food safety practices
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills

Barista

Dublin City Centre Part-time / Full-time €13–€16/hr + tips

We're looking for a barista who loves coffee and enjoys creating memorable experiences for our guests. If you enjoy interacting with people, staying organised, and working in a lively café environment, this role is perfect for you

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve coffee, tea, and specialty beverages to recipe standards
  • Maintain a clean and organized barista station, equipment, and dining area
  • Communicate with the team to ensure smooth service flow
  • Greet customers and provide friendly, efficient service

Requirements:

  • 1–2 years of barista or customer service experience preferred
  • Strong knowledge of coffee basics and willingness to learn new techniques
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment while maintaining quality

Front of House Server

Dublin City Centre Part-time / Full-time €12–€14/hr + tips

We're looking for energetic, service-focused team members who enjoy creating great guest experiences. The ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced environment, communicates clearly, pays attention to detail, and takes pride in delivering exceptional hospitality.

Responsibilities:

  • Greet guests and provide friendly, attentive service
  • Take orders accurately and deliver food and beverages promptly
  • Communicate effectively with the kitchen and bar teams
  • Process payments and follow opening/closing procedures as needed

Requirements:

  • 1–2 years of serving or customer service experience preferred
  • Strong communication and hospitality skills
  • Ability to multitask and stay composed in a fast-paced environment

Kitchen Porter

Dublin City Centre Part-time / Full-time €12–€15/hr + tips

We're looking for a hardworking Kitchen Porter to help keep our kitchen clean, organized, and running smoothly. If you're reliable, efficient, and enjoy being part of a fast-paced team, this role is a great fit

Responsibilities:

  • Wash dishes, utensils, pots, and pans efficiently
  • Keep kitchen areas clean, sanitized, and free of clutter
  • Assist with basic food prep when needed
  • Take out rubbish and manage recycling
  • Follow all hygiene and safety standards

Requirements:

  • Ability to work quickly and stay organized in a busy environment
  • Strong work ethic and attention to cleanliness
  • Team player with a positive attitude
  • No prior experience required—training provided
