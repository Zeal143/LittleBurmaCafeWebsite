Explore Our Signature Dishes

From traditional Burmese favorites to classic Irish comfort foods, made fresh every day

Burmese Cuisines

Mohinga

A bowl of rice noodles covered in a fish based soup and sprinkled with deep fried fritters

Tea Leaf Salad

A mix of fermented tea leaves tossed with crunchy beans, nuts, garlic, and sesame seeds

Shan Noodles

Rice noodles topped with spiced minced meat, pickled vegetables, and a light tomato-based broth

Coconut Noodle

Coconut chicken noodle soup topped with boiled egg, crispy fritters, and fresh garnishes

Shwe Yin Aye

A chilled dessert of coconut milk jelly, sago pearls, bread, and assorted toppings

Sanwin Makin
